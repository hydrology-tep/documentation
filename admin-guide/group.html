3.6. Groups management¶

The groups management page contains a list of all existing groups, with the possibility for an Administrator to Create a new group, Update or Delete an existing one.
The Administrator can also Manage users of a group (by clicking on ). This will allow him to add/remove users into/from a group.
To add a user in the group, click on in front of the user’s name in the All Users panel.
To remove a user from the group, click on in front of the user’s name in the Members panel.
